Players

John Terry

John Terry is a strong, tenacious, commanding and physical defender, Terry excels in the air and is known for his aggressive tackling, positioning and his ability to read the game;he is considered to be one of the best central defenders of his generation

Eden Hazard

Eden Hazard primarily plays as an attacking midfielder and a winger. Hazard's creativity, speed, and technical ability are widely acknowledged. He has been described as "a superb passer" and has earned critical acclaim for his playing style

Oscar

Oscar has drawn comparisons to players such as Mesut Özil and fellow Brazilian teammate Kaká due to his playmaking abilities. As a playmaker, Oscar has great vision and is able to deliver the precise pass to his teammates.His flexibility of being able to play in wider positions is also admired.

Diego Costa

Diego Costa has been described by pundits as a talented striker whose main attributes are his physicality, goalscoring and ability to keep possession, however he has also been criticised and punished for several confrontations with opponents

Radamel Falcao

Radamel Falcao on loan from French club Monaco, and captain of the Colombia national team. He is sometimes nicknamed El Tigre (Spanish for The Tiger) or King of the Europa League.Falcao has been described as one of the most "natural in form, goal scorers" in the world.

Willian

Willian A fast, agile, hard-working, and technically gifted player, Willian is known for his quick feet, acceleration, and explosive style of play, which allows him to dribble past opponents at speed whilst keeping close control of the ball typically Brazilian style, he has also been known to pull off feints and tricks, such as the elastico, in order to confuse and beat defenders.

Cesc Fabregas

Cesc Fabregas came through La Masia, Barcelona's youth academy, leaving at 16 when he was signed by English Premier League side Arsenal in September 2003.He broke several of the club's records in the process, earning a reputation as one of the best players in his position, and won the FA Cup in 2005.

Pedro

Pedro is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a forward or a winger for English club Chelsea and the Spain national team. During the 09/10 season, he became the first player in history to score in six different official club competitions in one season

Cahill

Cahill is known for his technical ability, powerful heading in defence and attack. The centre back also has an impressive goal-scoring record from defence. Former Chelsea manager Andre Villas-Boas said of Cahill, "I think his technical attributes are immense and his pace is fantastic."
